Question: Many stars can be seen in the sky at night. Which statement best explains why the Sun appears brighter than the stars seen in the night sky?
Answer: The Sun is closer to Earth than the stars seen in the night sky.

Question: Which process in plants is most similar to sexual reproduction in vertebrates?
Answer: cross-pollination

Question: John was using his mp3 player when it suddenly stopped working. What is the first thing John should do to try correcting the problem?
Answer: recharge the battery

Question: The heart is the major organ of the circulatory system. Which part of the body is responsible for delivering de-oxygenated blood to the heart?
Answer: veins

Question: Which is true about reproduction for both an amoeba and a paramecium?
Answer: A paramecium can conjugate, but an amoeba cannot.

Question: A student plans to design a new toothpaste tube that will give the same amount of toothpaste every time it is squeezed. Which should the student do first when designing the new tube?
Answer: draw a few possible solutions

Question: Which term best describes the life cycle of an insect that reaches the adult stage without being a pupa?
Answer: incomplete metamorphosis

Question: Many common hand tools create a mechanical advantage by using the basic principles found in simple machines. For example, a screwdriver uses the principles of the wheel and axle. In order to increase gripping force, pliers use the principle demonstrated in the
Answer: lever.

Question: A student adds water and detergent to a cup of soil. The mixture is shaken and allowed to settle. The student observes that silt particles stay suspended long after the other particles form layers at the bottom of the jar. The most likely explanation is that the silt particles are
Answer: less dense.

Question: A tuna is an ocean fish that is well adapted to catching small, fast-moving prey. Which of the following adaptations most helps a tuna swim fast to catch its prey?
Answer: large fins

Question: The students observed a large crack found in a boulder with a tree growing out of it. Which process were the students observing?
Answer: weathering

Question: Which event occurs every day?
Answer: Sun rising and setting

Question: The weight of heavy machinery compacts soil, especially when it is wet. Why do farmers avoid driving their machinery across wet ground?
Answer: Plants cannot grow when soil is compacted.

Question: Four identical candles are set on a safe surface and lit. One is covered with a small-sized jar, one is covered with a large-sized jar, and one candle is left in the open. A fourth lit candle is placed in a vacuum. Which candle will most likely stay lit the longest?
Answer: the candle left out in the open

Question: A student gives a red toy car a push on a wooden floor. The student then pushes the same red toy car on a cement floor. Which question is the student most likely investigating?
Answer: Which surface allows a toy car to roll farther?

Question: Which is true about the Sun and Earth?
Answer: The Sun gives light and heat energy to Earth.

Question: How do the sprouts of seeds buried in complete dark in the soil find their way to the surface of the soil?
Answer: They grow opposite the direction of the pull of gravity.

Question: Felicia noticed how air temperatures were cooler and there were fewer hours of daylight during some of the seasons. Which of the following contributes to these seasonal changes?
Answer: Earth revolves around the Sun.

Question: Which of the following distinguishes the organisms in the kingdom Fungi from other eukaryotic organisms?
Answer: Fungi obtain nutrients by absorption.

Question: Which is true of the seasonal weather patterns of places that are at the same latitude?
Answer: They have the same intensity of sunlight.

Question: The gravitational force of the Sun affects the planets in our solar system. Which of these is influenced the most by this force?
Answer: orbital path

Question: Which term is used to describe a physical property of a mineral?
Answer: solid

Question: Small mammals have many adaptations that keep them warm in winter. Which would not help conserve heat?
Answer: running

Question: The Ohio state tree is the buckeye. Which of these is a trait that is inherited by the buckeye tree in the reproduction process?
Answer: change in leaf color during the autumn season

Question: Which statement best describes a neutron?
Answer: It adds mass to the nucleus.
